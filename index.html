MANDATORY THESIS MEETING

Please be advised of the mandatory thesis meeting for Ph.D. and M.S. candidates completing a thesis and who plan to complete degree requirements in spring 2019.

Mandatory Thesis Meeting
Date: Monday, February 11th, 2019
Time: 5-6 p.m.
Where: Siegel Hall, Rm. 118 Auditorium
Registration: Not Required

Students completing a thesis are required to attend the Mandatory Thesis Meeting a minimum of one time. Additional attendance is optional, but open to all candidates. However, there are no make-up meetings, as it occurs only once per semester. Therefore, first-time attendees with a date conflict or who are unable to attend should contact the Director of Academic Writing and Thesis Examiner, Dr. Patty Johnson-Winston, in advance at winston@iit.edu for special arrangements.

Other Important Semester Deadlines for Degree Candidates (including examination and appointment deadlines)

All degree candidates must be registered in a minimum of one credit hour in the semester of graduation. If you are not registered, contact Graduate Academic Affairs for more information. The Office of Graduate Academic Affairs is available to assist you with questions from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, gradcoll@iit.edu, (312) 567-3024.
